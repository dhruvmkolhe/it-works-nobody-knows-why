## 🎲 Step 1 — Choose a Style

Randomly select **one** design style from the list below _(or choose a
professional style of your own that fits better)_. You must use a genuinely
random method — do not default to Neobrutalist or any favorite. **Randomize each
time.**

Then write **exactly three paragraphs**, as described in
[Step 2](#-step-2--write-the-three-paragraphs).

## ✍️ Step 2 — Write the Three Paragraphs

### Paragraph 1 · Concept & Mood

State the randomly chosen style. Then ask the AI to invent an **original
business or service concept** for a single-page landing page. Describe the
emotional qualities and atmosphere this style should create:

- What should visitors **feel** the moment they arrive?
- How should the visual hierarchy and scrolling flow make them feel as they move
  down this single, cohesive page?
- Include a note to use **colorful elements thoughtfully** to enhance emotional
  impact.

---

### Paragraph 2 · Emotional UX & Journey

Explain the design philosophy through **emotion and user experience**:

- How should typography **feel**? _(e.g., authoritative, welcoming,
  cutting-edge)_
- What sensation should interactions and animations create? _(e.g., smooth and
  liquid, snappy and precise, gentle and organic)_
- Describe how the single-page scrolling journey should **emotionally progress**
  from first impression to final call-to-action, forming a complete narrative
  arc.

---

### Paragraph 3 · Abstract References & Inspirations

Provide abstract reference points that capture the aesthetic's essence:

- The feeling of certain **spaces, cultural movements, artistic periods,
  architectural styles**, or design philosophies.
- The emotional qualities of **premium experiences**, sophisticated
  environments, or refined craftsmanship that should inspire the design.
- Explain how these abstract references should shape the **emotional tone and
  visual sophistication** of the final single-page design — _without naming
  specific brands or platforms_.

---

## ⚠️ Final Reminder

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The output must be a prompt for **ONE COHESIVE LANDING PAGE** with a single
> scrolling experience.
>
> - Focus on **feeling, atmosphere, and abstract quality references**.
> - Avoid technical specs or specific brand examples.
> - Keep everything **conceptual** to allow maximum creative freedom.
